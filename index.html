Andrés E. Rentería-Olivo

PhD Candidate in Theoretical Particle Physics

I am a Ph.D. student at the Instituto de Física Corpuscular (IFIC) in Valencia, Spain. My research focuses on precision calculations for scattering amplitudes, quantum computing, and machine learning applications in high-energy physics.

With a solid background in theoretical physics, I am passionate about advancing our understanding of the quantum realm and contributing to the next generation of computational methods in physics.

Research Interests

My current research areas include:

  • Precision Calculations for Multi-Loop Scattering Amplitudes
  • Quantum Algorithms for Feynman Diagram Evaluation
  • Applications of Machine Learning in High-Energy Physics
  • Development of Computational Methods for Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD)

Selected Publications

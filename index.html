About Me

I am a physicist specialising in precision high-energy physics, with a particular focus on the theoretical development of Quantum Field Theories, Quantum Algorithms, Machine Learning and Data Analysis. My research interests span from the development of theoretical techniques for precision particle physics to the application of quantum computing in high-energy physics, concretely in addressing complex problems related perturbative Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD), such as multi-loop, multi-leg scattering amplitudes and Feynman integrals and the development of quantum algorithms. .
